Key Features of Our Teleradiology Services

Rapid Turnaround Time

At Global Teleradiology Services, we understand that time is critical in patient care. We ensure high-quality radiology reporting with:
A standard turnaround time of 90 minutes and
Emergency scans delivered within 45 minutes

Hassle-Free Technical Platform

Our services are powered by a state-of-the-art PACS system, offering:
Seamless and secure data handling
Full backend technical support
Automated monitoring of turnaround times through software and alert systems

Experienced Radiology Team

We maintain the highest standards in reporting by:
Conducting rigorous credential verification before onboarding teleradiologists
Performing regular clinical audits and review meetings
Implementing a system for tracking and managing reporting discrepancies

Flexible and Adaptive Services

We tailor our services to meet institutional operational needs. With integrated PACS and dedicated technical support, we offer flexible solution that adapts to the workflow — ensuring smooth coordination between your team and ours.

Bony structures
Tumor positioning
Vascular pathways
Angiographic studies
These visuals aid physicians in making precise, informed treatment decisions.
